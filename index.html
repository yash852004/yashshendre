Projects

Projects

My Recent Projects.

IOT Based Energy Management System

This project focuses on optimizing energy usage in residential or commercial settings by using IoT technology. The system automatically controls room appliances like lights and fans based on occupancy and environmental conditions (light and temperature). Using sensors such as IR, LDR, and DHT11, along with an Arduino UNO, the system ensures energy conservation by switching off appliances when rooms are unoccupied. This project demonstrates the practical application of IoT in reducing energy waste and improving efficiency..

SCOPE OF EXTRACTION UNIT FOR MEDICAL AND AGRICULTURAL PURPOSE BY STEAM DISTILLATION PROCESS.

This project focuses on developing a cost-effective and eco-friendly extraction unit using the steam distillation process. The system is designed to efficiently extract essential oils and other valuable plant materials from various herbs and leaves like eucalyptus, marigold, and rose. This method provides farmers and pharmaceutical industries with a scalable solution for producing extracts for fertilizers, medicines, and other applications, reducing both operational costs and time.

Pick and Place Robot

This project involves designing and developing an automated Pick and Place Robot that can efficiently lift and move objects from one location to another. The robot is equipped with sensors and actuators for precise control, making it ideal for industrial applications like assembly lines and warehouses. It reduces manual labor and enhances productivity by automating repetitive tasks with accuracy and speed.
